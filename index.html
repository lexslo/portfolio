About Me

  • I am a developer, sound engineer, and composer with 6+ years of experience working in Higher Education and 10+ years of customer service experience.
  • I love to implement experimental sound design principles in a technical environment to expand the horizons of what is possible in the audio world.
  • Some of my performances have included the use of Wii remotes, a Makey Makey, video game controllers, and house lamps - just to name a few - to generate MIDI data and create interesting visual complements to the sonic experience.
  • My goal is to combine creativity and logic in order to design new and exciting tools for the enjoyment of humanity.
